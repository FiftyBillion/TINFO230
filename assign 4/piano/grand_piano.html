Introduce Grand Piano

Believe it or not, the grand piano was the basic original shape of the first pianos that were invented. The models for these shapes were the harpsichord and clavichord, two instruments of an earlier time. Here are some of the more commonly known quality brands: Bosendorfer (Viennese, founded in 1828), Steinway and Sons (American, founded in 1853), Baldwin Company (American, founded in 1862), Bechstein (German, founded in 1856), Yamaha (Japan, founded in 1887, first piano in 1900), Heintzman and Co. Ltd. (Canadian, founded in 1866). The list of reputable piano manufacturers can go on and on, but even quality brands are not a guarantee of a good piano. Even the best brands can suffer the wear and tear of time and abuse.
